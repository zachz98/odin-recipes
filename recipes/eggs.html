Eggs

Description

Scramble Eggs is one of the most simple dish to cook!

Some eggs and a pan is all you need!

Ingredients

  • 3 jumbo eggs
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix 3 eggs
  2. Pre-heat the pan using medium fire
  3. Put some oil to the heated pan and just add the mixed eggs into the pan
  4. Scramble for 2 minutes and serve
