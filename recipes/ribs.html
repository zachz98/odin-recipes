Ribs

Description

Making ribs is simple - first of course you need to get some ribs, also the sauce.

You just need to season the ribs in the sauce and put it into the oven - which is set to 450 degrees - and wait for 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of ribs
  • Hickory Sauce
  • Salt
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Season the ribs with Hickory sauce and salt
  2. Pre-heat the oven to 450 degrees
  3. After seasoned for 30 minutes put it into the heated oven
  4. Bake for 30 minutes and serve
