Steak

Description

Steak is one of the most simple dish to cook!

Season it or not depending on your preferences, and you just need a pan to cook it.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of the Steak of your choice
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Thyme
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Season the steak if you want with salt
  2. Pre-heat the pan using high fire
  3. After seasoned for 30 minutes put it into the pan
  4. Sear for 5 minutes each side and serve
